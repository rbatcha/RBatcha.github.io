CRUEL IRONY! Mega America's Households Are Food Insecure: Empty pantries, Junk pantries

Food security and Insecurity are social concerns I love creating awareness on and proposing solutions to solving them. I was tasked to create a long Infographic as a class assignment, from a bivariate or multivariate data. I examined food insecurity in mega America. While examining the concept of food insecurity, I gained knowledge in contextualizing and broadening the definition to include obesity. In America, high prevalence of obesity is key in defining food insecurity. This infographic tells an ironic story of the Americas situation. In every five persons in Mega America, one person is suffering from food insecurity!
